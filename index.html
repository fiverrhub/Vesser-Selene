About Vesser Selene

A Rising Star in Storytelling

Vesser Selene is a passionate writer whose commitment to storytelling has made her a standout in the creative community. She joined Fiverr in late 2023, and her journey has been nothing short of remarkable.

Achievements in Contests

In 2024, Vesser participated in the Fiverr Steel-a-Palooza Contest, submitting three of her original stories. Her talent shone brightly, with two of her entries ranking in the top 100 and one breaking into the top 50.

Her skill extends beyond storytelling—Vesser is a master of wording illustration, an ability to paint vivid and immersive scenes through her prose. This talent was evident in the Fiverr Fiction Fest Contest, where one of the stories she assisted ranked in the top 6. Notably, this contest featured contributions from the top 10 writers from the Palooza event, underscoring her collaborative prowess and creative excellence.

Global Recognition

Vesser Selene's dedication and talent earned global recognition when her story Bound by Ink stunned the Fiverr community by ranking in the top 30 of the Fiverr Inspiration Awards 2024. Notably, she became one of only eight Fiverr clients to achieve this prestigious ranking in the competition. This accomplishment solidified her status as one of the brightest newcomers on the platform.

Elite Status

In light of her incredible journey, Fiverr has honored Vesser Selene by inducting her into the Fiverr Elite Member Club. Her dedication, creativity, and meteoric rise serve as an inspiration to storytellers everywhere.

We wish Vesser Selene continued success as she leaves an indelible mark on the world of storytelling!
